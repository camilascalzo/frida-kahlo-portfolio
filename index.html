I am Frida Kahlo

People call me the master of self-portrait.

This might be true. But I leave my soul in every canvas, not because of pride, but because I am often alone… so alone that I become the person I know best. I have no excuses.

People also call me surrealist, but I am not such a thing. I don’t paint my dreams (or anyone else's dreams) … I paint my reality. The reality of what my eyes see and my heart feels.

My paintings carry the weight of my own pain and miseries. They carry life as it is. I lost many things in my life, so they took that empty space and made it their own.

I used to think I was the strangest person in the world but then I realized there are so many people in the world, there must be someone just like me who feels bizarre and flawed in the same ways I do. I imagine they must be out there thinking about me as well.

I hope that if you are out there and read this you understand that I'm here, I'm just as strange as you and I’m the right person for the job.
